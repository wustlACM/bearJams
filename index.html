WashU's first Game Jam

A Game Jam is like a hackathon but for Games! Do you love playing games? Want to find out more about how they are made? Interested in being a GameDev but don't know where to start? Then this event is made for you!

The art of making games requires much more than just coding, from Designers to Artists and Writers, it takes more than Programmers to make a game. We hope to garner greater inter-school collaboration and interest in the WashU community through making games and bringing friends together.
